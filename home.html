Get into the Green Scene

Grow Green is a social enterprise aiming to create a positive change in the environment by promoting sustainable products which efficiently replaces products made from and packaged with plastics. Our products are made with love from bamboo andare unbleached, thus not harming the environment from any angle. The packaging of our products are made from recycled paper, which is biodegradable and even compostable.

We work towards the issue of sustainability from many angles. We have found that nearly everything we touch can be replaced by a more eco-friendly product that does not have to go to the landfills or be incarcerated - both toxic for the environment, but can be turned into compost in your own backyard.

Our platform also aims to change the way our society shops by eliminating plastics from packaging, even though the product is offered with greater convenience and style.

For every product that you purchase from us, a part of your money will go towards equipping rural and marginalized households with sustainable alternative technologies which replaces firewood as the fuel to cook.

  • Strong and flexible. The reasons bamboo is widely used is its strength and flexibility. As furniture materials, bamboo has the ability to bend and is comfortable to sit on.

  • Easy to maintain. All types of bamboo products are usually very smooth due to its natural oil coating. Bamboo is also very easy to clean.

History

Grow Green was founded in September 2012, on a mission towards a green and sustainable way of life. From these humble beginnings, Grow Green has grown to be the national leader in advocating the use of sustainable products.

Our Vision

We believe that all of us have the right to live in a greener, cleaner and healthier world sustainably. We envision a carbon negative future where each individual plays their part to decrease the carbon footprint that the world as a whole has been leaving behind. We are on a mission to drive a positive change in the world, starting with our society, towards sustainability with eco-friendly alternatives to single-use plastics and packaging.
